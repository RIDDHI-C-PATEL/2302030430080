How It Works

Registration

Participants register for the event, providing information about the skills they have to offer and the skills they are interested in learning.

Event Day

On the day of the event, participants gather at the designated venue where various skill exchange booths or stations are set up.

Skill Sharing

Participants visit different booths or stations to engage in skill sharing activities. They have the opportunity to teach skills they are proficient in and learn new skills from others in the community.

Workshops and Demonstrations

Throughout the day, workshops and demonstrations are held on a variety of topics, allowing participants to dive deeper into specific skills or areas of interest.

Networking and Connection

Participants have the chance to network and connect with others who share similar interests or complementary skills, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Feedback and Reflection

After the event, participants may provide feedback on their experience, helping organizers improve future skill exchange fairs.
